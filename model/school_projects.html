SCHOOL PROJECTS

The school where I study follows a PBL (Project Base Learning) pedagogy, which means that we learn new notions by manipulating and experimenting. As a result, we learn about different areas of IT, such as software development and information systems administration. We learn new concepts by working on a project, which is then presented as if we were engineers presenting their work to a client who has made a request. This work methodology enables us to develop our technical skills by manipulating, but also our quality of analysis and reflection by confronting a problem and looking for solutions. Here are a few examples of projects I've carried out at the school.

SYSTEM ADMINISTRATION

Responsible for updating a company's entire information system to ensure compliance with standards and integration with another company.

SYSTEM PROGRAMMING

Development of software designed to create backup programs, which can then be launched with various parameters, such as prioritized files and encryption options.

SYSTEM AND NETWORK


Implementation of a simulation of a city's computer network.

WEB DEVELOPMENT

Development of an internship search website for the CESI school to help students in their search.

EMBEDDED SYSTEM

Creation of a miniature weather station to collect meteorological data for further study.
